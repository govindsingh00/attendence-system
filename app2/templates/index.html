How It Works

Simple steps to modernize attendance in your college.

1. Register

Admins add teachers and students. Users upload their photo for face enrollment.

2. Capture

Teachers open the attendance session and the system detects faces in real time.

3. Verify

Each face is matched against enrolled profiles and attendance is marked instantly.

4. Analyze

View detailed reports, export data, and track attendance trends anytime.
